Axiom Layer connects to your identity provider, MDM, email, and financial systems to build a complete IT inventory automatically. You get spend visibility, renewal tracking, and compliance readiness without spreadsheets or manual data entry.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your tools">
    Link your identity provider, MDM, and email systems. Axiom Layer uses read-only API access and never modifies your data.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Automatic discovery">
    The multi-source discovery engine finds every SaaS app, device, and license across SSO logs, OAuth grants, billing emails, and MDM data.
  </Step>
  <Step title="See the full picture">
    Your dashboard shows spend, renewals, compliance gaps, and shadow IT, updated continuously.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## The Axiom platform

Axiom Layer is the foundation of the Axiom platform. It feeds asset and vendor data into companion products:

- **Axiom Drift** -- SaaS discovery and shadow IT detection
- **Axiom Codex** -- compliance automation and control management

---
title: Google Workspace
description: Connect Google Workspace to sync users, groups, and admin audit logs into Axiom Layer with one-click OAuth.
---

The Google Workspace integration uses OAuth to connect your directory to Axiom Layer. A super-admin signs in once, grants read-only consent, and Axiom Layer syncs your users, groups, and audit logs automatically. No GCP project or service account setup required.

## Prerequisites

- A Google Workspace account with **super-admin** privileges.
- Access to the Axiom Layer **Integrations** page (requires admin role in your organization).

## What gets synced

| Data | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Users | Directory users including email, name, status, and 2FA enrollment |
| Groups | Google Workspace groups and their memberships |
| Admin audit logs | Sign-in events, admin actions, and security-related activity |
| Drive sharing | External sharing and permissions data |

All data is read-only. Axiom Layer never modifies your Google Workspace configuration.

## Connect Google Workspace

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open integrations">
    Go to **Integrations** in the Axiom Layer sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Google Workspace">
    Locate the **Google Workspace** card under **User management and IAM** and click **Connect**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in and grant consent">
    Click **Sign in with Google Workspace** in the dialog. You are redirected to Google's consent screen.

    Grant the requested permissions:
    - Read-only access to directory users
    - Read-only access to directory groups
    - Read-only access to admin audit reports

    <Note>
      You must sign in with a super-admin account. Non-admin accounts cannot grant the required directory permissions.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm connection">
    After granting consent, you are redirected back to Axiom Layer. A confirmation banner appears and the first sync starts automatically.

    Refresh the page in a few minutes to see your synced data.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Permissions requested

Axiom Layer requests the following Google OAuth scopes:

| Scope | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| `admin.directory.user.readonly` | Read user profiles and account status |
| `admin.directory.group.readonly` | Read groups and group memberships |
| `admin.reports.audit.readonly` | Read admin audit and sign-in activity logs |

These are all read-only scopes. Axiom Layer cannot create, modify, or delete users, groups, or settings in your Google Workspace.

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Connection fails with 'no access token'">
    Make sure you granted all requested permissions on the Google consent screen. If you skipped or denied a permission, disconnect and reconnect to try again.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="No data appears after connecting">
    The first sync can take a few minutes depending on directory size. If no data appears after 10 minutes, check the connection status on the **Integrations** page. If the status shows **Failing**, disconnect and reconnect.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="'Requires super-admin' error">
    The account you signed in with doesn't have super-admin privileges in Google Workspace. Ask your Google Workspace administrator to either grant you super-admin access or connect the integration themselves.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

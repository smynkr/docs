title: Renewals and contracts

---

Axiom Layer centralizes your SaaS contracts and surfaces upcoming renewals so you never miss a negotiation window.

## Contract management

### Upload a contract

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to contracts">
    Go to **Contracts** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload your contract">
    Select **Upload contract** and drag in a PDF or image of your SaaS agreement. Axiom Layer's AI extracts key terms including vendor name, renewal date, pricing tiers, auto-renewal clauses, and contract duration.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review extracted data">
    After extraction, review the parsed fields and make corrections if needed. The contract is linked to the corresponding app in your asset inventory.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>You can also forward contract emails directly to Axiom Layer for automatic extraction.</Tip>

### Manual contract entry

If you prefer not to upload a document, you can create a contract record manually with fields for vendor, start date, end date, value, and renewal terms.

## Renewal calendar

The **Renewals** page displays a calendar view of upcoming contract renewals. Each entry shows:

- Vendor name and contract value
- Days until renewal
- Renewal urgency (color-coded by proximity)

## Renewal alerts

Axiom Layer sends automated alerts before contracts renew:

| Alert | Timing |
|---|---|
| Early warning | 60 days before renewal |
| Action needed | 30 days before renewal |
| Urgent | 7 days before renewal |

Configure alert delivery channels (email, Slack, or both) in **Settings** > **Notifications**.

### Renewal recommendations

For each upcoming renewal, Axiom Layer provides AI-generated recommendations based on:

- Current license utilization rates
- Spend benchmarks against similar organizations
- Historical usage trends

These help you decide whether to renew, downgrade, or cancel before the auto-renewal window closes.

## Renewal feed

Export your renewal calendar as an ICS feed at **Renewals** > **Export** to subscribe from any calendar application (Google Calendar, Outlook, Apple Calendar). You can also export renewal data as CSV for reporting.

title: Spend management
description: Track SaaS spend across vendors, departments, and categories. Identify unused licenses, redundant tools, and optimization opportunities.

Axiom Layer aggregates spend data from financial integrations, contract uploads, and email receipts to give you a unified view of your SaaS costs.

## Spend sources

Axiom Layer collects spend data from multiple sources:

| Source | How it works |
|---|---|
| Corporate cards | Transactions from Brex, Ramp, and other financial integrations are automatically matched to vendors |
| Email receipts | Invoice and receipt emails are parsed from Gmail and Microsoft 365 Mail |
| Contracts | Uploaded contracts with AI-extracted pricing data |
| Manual entry | Manually added spend records for tools not covered by integrations |

## Dashboard

The spend dashboard at **Spend** in the sidebar shows:

- **Total spend** by month, quarter, or year
- **Spend by vendor** with trends over time
- **Spend by category** (productivity, security, infrastructure, etc.)
- **Per-app detail** pages with historical spend and license counts

## License optimization

Axiom Layer identifies opportunities to reduce spend:

- **Unused licenses** -- licenses assigned to employees who haven't used the tool within a configurable period
- **Redundant tools** -- AI-powered detection of overlapping tools (for example, multiple project management or password manager subscriptions)
- **License harvesting** -- recommendations to reclaim unused seats before the next renewal

The license review queue at **Licenses** > **Review queue** surfaces actionable recommendations with estimated savings.

## Budgets

Set budgets to track spend against targets:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a budget">
    Go to **Spend** > **Budgets** and select **Add budget**. Choose a scope (category, department, or specific app) and set a monthly or annual limit.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Monitor progress">
    The budgets page shows current spend against each budget with visual progress indicators. Budgets that exceed their threshold are flagged.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Virtual cards

If you use Stripe Issuing, Axiom Layer can issue per-vendor virtual cards that enforce spend policies at the point of purchase. Virtual cards are managed from **Spend** > **Virtual cards**.

Each card is scoped to a specific vendor and includes:

- **Spend limits** per transaction or per month
- **Automatic approval** or manual review workflows
- **Real-time transaction tracking** in the spend dashboard

## Benchmarking

The **Benchmarks** page compares your spend against aggregated, anonymized data from similar organizations. You can see how your per-employee costs for common tools compare to industry medians and identify areas where you may be overpaying.

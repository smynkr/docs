<Update label="2026-04-21" description="Week of April 14–18" tags={["Feature", "Update"]}>

## New features

**Structured permit work descriptions** — Building permits now include LLM-extracted work descriptions, making it easier to understand what each permit covers without reading the raw filing. This data appears in cell detail views and CSV exports.

**OSHA severe injury data** — Over 200,000 OSHA Severe Injury Reports are now ingested and factored into safety scoring. This adds a new layer of workplace safety signal alongside the existing OSHA inspection data.

**Scout digest data previews** — The [Scout weekly digest](/scout/discovery-triage) now includes a sample of each discovered source's data, so you can evaluate relevance directly from the email without opening the dataset.

**Health and employment signals** — Two new signal types — health indicators and employment density — are now available in cell scoring, giving you a fuller picture of neighborhood dynamics.

**Supply Chain Risk plan tier** — A new Supply Chain Risk plan is available for teams focused on logistics and supply chain exposure analysis.

## Updates

**Adaptive scoring resolution** — Cell scoring now uses higher-resolution H3 cells in densely populated areas and coarser resolution in rural zones. This means scores are more precise where detail matters most.

**12 new data source loaders** — Scout now collects from 12 additional public data sources, broadening coverage across civic records, permits, and environmental data.

</Update>

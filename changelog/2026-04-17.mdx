<Update label="2026-04-17" tags={["Feature"]}>
  ## One-click OAuth for Google Workspace and Microsoft 365

  Connecting Google Workspace and Microsoft 365 to Axiom Layer is now significantly easier. Instead of manually creating service accounts or registering Azure AD apps, you can now connect with a single OAuth sign-in.

  **What changed:**

  - **Google Workspace** and **Microsoft 365** integrations now use an OAuth consent flow. Click **Connect**, sign in with your admin account, grant consent, and you're done.
  - No more creating GCP projects, generating service-account keys, or registering apps in Azure AD.
  - Existing credential-based connections continue to work — no migration required.

  This means faster setup, fewer configuration steps, and no need to involve your cloud infrastructure team just to connect an identity provider.
</Update>

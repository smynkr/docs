## New features

**One-click OAuth for Intune, Teams, and M365 Mail** — The Microsoft Intune, Microsoft Teams, and Microsoft 365 Mail connectors now use the same one-click OAuth sign-in flow as Google Workspace and Microsoft Entra ID. You no longer need to enter tenant IDs, client secrets, or access tokens manually — just click **Connect**, sign in with your Microsoft work account, and grant consent. Each connector shows the required admin role (Intune Administrator, Teams Administrator, or Exchange Administrator) so the right person on your team can complete setup.

## Updates

**Scout weekly digest delivered by email** — The Scout weekly digest is now sent as a formatted HTML email instead of a Slack message. You no longer need to configure a Slack webhook — just set your delivery email address and digests arrive in your inbox every Monday. See [Scout discovery triage](/scout/discovery-triage) for setup details.

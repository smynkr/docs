This guide walks you through connecting your first integration and viewing your IT asset inventory.

## Prerequisites

- An Axiom Layer account ([sign up](https://axiomlayer.com/signup) for a free tier with up to 25 employees)
- Admin access to at least one of: your identity provider, MDM platform, or email system

## Set up your account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your organization">
    Sign up at [axiomlayer.com](https://axiomlayer.com/signup) and follow the onboarding flow to name your organization. You can sign up with Google or email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your first integration">
    Navigate to **Settings** and select an integration to connect. For the fastest results, start with your identity provider (Okta, Google Workspace, or Microsoft Entra ID) since it provides the broadest visibility into SaaS usage.

    Each integration uses read-only API access. Axiom Layer never modifies your data.

    <Tip>Google Workspace is included on the free tier and provides SSO logs, OAuth grants, and directory data in a single connection.</Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run initial discovery">
    After connecting, Axiom Layer begins scanning automatically. Your first asset inventory typically appears within 5 minutes. You can monitor progress from the **Dashboard**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Explore your inventory

Once discovery completes, your dashboard shows:

- **Apps** -- every SaaS application detected across your connected sources
- **People** -- employees and their application assignments
- **Hardware** -- devices pulled from MDM integrations (Jamf, Intune, Kandji, and others)
- **Renewals** -- upcoming contract renewal dates with alert timelines
